About

DWELL is a Brussels-based studio specialising in cinematic marketing for premium properties. We combine spatial intelligence with film craft to create experiences that make people feel a space before they ever visit.

Architect & digital strategist

Architect and AI specialist. Matteo brings spatial intelligence and computational design thinking to every project — translating physical architecture into immersive digital experiences.

Filmmaker and droneist. Romeo captures the soul of each space through cinematic storytelling — from sweeping aerial perspectives to intimate stop-motion room studies.
