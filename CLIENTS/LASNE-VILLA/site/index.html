THE PROPERTY

Where Precision
Meets
Wilderness

Completed in 2019, Villa Caturia occupies 807 m² of meticulously crafted living space across a wooded estate of 1.56 hectares in Lasne — one of Wallonia's most sought-after addresses. Its single-floor architecture is deliberately low, pressing itself gently into the topography as if always meant to be there.

The landscape was designed by Delvosalle, whose approach treats greenery with the same rigor as the built form. Each tree, each sightline, each terrace has been considered as part of a unified composition.
