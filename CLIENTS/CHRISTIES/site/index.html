The Property
"A residence that sets its own definition of space."

Set within three hectares of landscaped grounds in the most sought-after commune south of Brussels, Villa des Érables was conceived as a retreat — a place where architecture recedes in favour of the landscape it frames.

The interiors unfold across 485 m² of considered living space, where each room is oriented around a view, a material, or a quality of light. The renovation completed in 2024 preserved the original proportions while introducing a new standard of finish.

A 20-metre pool, a separate guest cottage, and direct access to the Lasne valley complete a property of rare completeness.
