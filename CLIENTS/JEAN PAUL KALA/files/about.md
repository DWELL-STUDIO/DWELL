## Vision

Art is beauty. Beauty is art.

Jean-Paul Kala is never resting — always producing, always feeding his inner self with every bit of beauty he can find in life.

## Biography

The refined works of Jean-Paul Kala are an ode to the powerful and authentic nature of his birthplace: **North Kivu**, where he was born.

The budding artist was drawn to matter and mechanics, and his hands would not hold still. As a teenager, he discovered metal welding and shaping — a revelation that took over his whole being. He became a recognised expert in the field.

The European dream had been part of his plans for years. In the 2000s, he moved to **Belgium**, where he perfected his technique alongside the best in the field, earning both his peers' and the most demanding aficionados' recognition.

Probably partly influenced by the ancestral art of **Imigongo** — a precursor to abstract art — in 2013 he launched his own brand of high-end furniture, ***R&J***, which quickly acquired notoriety among enthusiasts on the lookout for the beautiful and exclusive.

Jean-Paul Kala's designs stand out for their organic, elegant, and generous curves — no doubt a product of his African origins. Through his technical mastery, paired with an acute attention to detail, he transforms metal into masterful works of contemporary art.

## In the studio

Each piece begins in his Belgian workshop: a sketch, then sheets of stainless steel cut, bent, and welded by hand. Nothing is cast or moulded. Every curve is shaped, every weld is visible — the work carries the trace of the maker.

![Parda — work in progress in the studio](https://jeanpaulkala.be/wp-content/uploads/2023/08/pardaJPHI_1.jpg)

## Exhibited worldwide

Jean-Paul Kala's pieces are exhibited in private and gallery settings across Europe and the Middle East — Brussels, Paris, Cannes, Dubai, Courchevel, and beyond. Collectors include private buyers and design-led interiors from Marbella to the Gulf.
